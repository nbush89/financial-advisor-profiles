About this firm

AJF Financial is a unique group of financial planners and financial advisors in New York focused on sustainable investing. Each stock, fund and bond in its investment pool must meet the company’s strict Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) criteria before inclusion. AJF’s team of reviewers doesn’t only evaluate assets based on the financial strength and potential for growth — it also considers data like carbon emissions, women in leadership positions and employee safety when it chooses where to invest your money. If you’re looking for a socially-conscious way to invest for the future, consider AJF’s financial advising and planning services.
